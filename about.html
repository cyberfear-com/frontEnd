Mission

Our mission is to make emails private again.
Privacy is under a lot of pressure.
Many powerful entities, such as NSA, are focused on erasing privacy worldwide.
The good news is, that just as many, if not more businesses, journalists, activists and computer specialists are committed to keeping the Internet a safe place free from surveillance.

Reason

We created Mailum because we could not find an email service which would stand up to our expectations.
We wanted to have an absolute certainty that our emails remain private and inaccessible to third party.
Unfortunately, every service we looked at, had some crucial drawbacks. It was either incomplete encryption, IP-logs, custodial keys (service operators could decrypt user's emails) or were closed-source.
Having a programming and security background, we decided to create Mailum.
